India

A country with a rich cultural heritage, a diverse population, and a variety of geographical features

India is one of the world's oldest civilizations with a rich cultural heritage

Bordered by the Himalayas, the Bay of Bengal, the Arabian Sea and the Indian Ocean

Travel Tips:

  • Learn local etiquette: Be aware that India is a conservative country.
  • Travel insurance: Get comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical expenses
  • Plan your communications: Plan your communications before you travel.

Destinations in India

Marina Beach

  • A natural urban beach in Chennai, Tamil Nadu
  • Spanning around 13 km along the Bay of Bengal
  • Known for its golden sand and shimmering water.

India Gate

  • The India Gate is a war memorial located near the Rajpath
  • At the centre of New Delhi stands a 42m high India Gate, an “Arc-de-Triomphe” like archway in the middle of a crossroad
  • Commemorates the 70,000 Indian soldiers who lost their lives fighting for the British Army during the World War I.
