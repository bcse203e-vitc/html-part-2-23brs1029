The Metamorphosis

Author: Frans Kafka | Date: September 16, 1903

Introduction

Wrote a few poems that were included in a letter to his school friend Oskar Pollak in 1903.

In the letter, Kafka asked Pollak to read the poems at his leisure.

Key Takeaways

  • Kafka deals with modernist themes such as isolation and the absurdity of life
  • Gregor has devoted himself to his family
  • The absurd situation of becoming an insect has left him alienated from other humans.
